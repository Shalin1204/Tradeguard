System Architecture

TradeGuard X — intent-enforced 4-layer enforcement pipeline

01
Pre-Policy Risk Gating
Risk evaluated before policy rules. Unsafe intent is blocked before rules even run — catches what rules can't.
02
Intent as Contract
Intent becomes a cryptographic execution boundary. Any action outside the contract scope is impossible to execute.
03
Fail-Closed Zero-Trust
Nothing executes unless explicitly verified. Errors, unknowns, and ambiguity all default to BLOCK.
04
Scoped Delegation
Each agent has non-overlapping capabilities. Research agents literally cannot execute trades — enforced at runtime.
